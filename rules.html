✨ NexusMC Server Rules ✨

🚫 1. Swearing 🤬
Swearing is allowed, but NOT if it's directed at a person. Violating this may result in a timeout!

❌ 2. No NSFW Content 👀
Posting NSFW content will result in a permanent ban. Keep it clean!

❌ 3. No Porn 🚷
Posting pornographic content = Permanent ban. No exceptions!

💌 4. No Dating
Dating is NOT allowed on the server. This can lead to a permanent ban.

🚫 5. No Asking for Promotions/Ranks 🔼
Do NOT ask for promotions or ranks. Apply through our Discord application system. Asking may result in a mute.

🚨 6. Don’t Argue with Higher-Ups 🗣️
If you have an issue or suggestion, open a ticket in our Discord server. Do NOT argue with staff.

⛔ 7. No Glitch Abuse 🛑
Exploiting glitches is forbidden! Play fair and keep survival fun! 🚫🕹️

⚠️ 8. No Mods That Give Unfair Advantages 👾
Mods like Freecam, World/Minimaps, and Tweakeroo are banned! We only allow a pure survival experience. Keep it fair! 🖥️🙅

👮 9. Respect ALL Staff & Members 👑
Mods and admins are here to help! Be respectful – they’re doing their best to keep NexusMC running smoothly! ❤️

🎰 10. No Gambling or Betting (Real Money) 💰
Gambling with real money or valuable items is NOT allowed. Let’s keep it fun and risk-free! 🚫💵

🗣️ 11. Be Respectful – Don’t Annoy Others 😇
Respect everyone. No trolling, spamming, or annoying players. Keep it friendly! ✋😊
